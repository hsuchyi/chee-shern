Olivia Sia

A dialogue editor, sound engineer, and sound designer who brings stories to life through sound. Whether it’s a film, game, or branded content, she shapes immersive sonic worlds that move people—clean, crisp, and emotionally charged.

Dialogue cleaning
Olivia specializes in polishing game dialogue to a professional standard using industry-leading tools like Adobe Audition and iZotope RX. From deep cleaning noisy recordings to restoring clarity and presence, she ensures every voice line sounds clean, consistent, and ready for immersive storytelling.
Sound editing and design
She designs custom soundscapes for films and advertisements using industry-standard software such as Logic Pro X and Pro Tools. With a focus on enhancing narrative and visual impact, her work ranges from detailed ambient environments to bold, creative sound effects that support and elevate the story.
Recording
She brings studio experience in recording voiceovers, audiobooks, and dubbing sessions. With a strong understanding of vocal performance and technical workflow, she ensures every session runs smoothly and delivers professional-grade audio with clarity and consistency.

Highlights

Nominated Most Anticipated Horror Game in 2024, Olivia was involved in dialogue editing for Tormented Souls 2.
Winner of Best Audio Accomplishment in the Pocket Gamer Mobile Games Awards 2025. Olivia was involved in dialogue editing for Delta Force Mobile.
