Audio Engineer

Ngan Hong

Based in Selangor, Chan Ngan Hong is an audio engineer who brings stories to life through sound, whether in the studio, on location, or in live environments. From mixing behind the console to holding the boom in the field, he weaves sonic textures that elevate narrative and emotion.

Mixing & Mastering
Ngan Hong specializes in mixing and mastering for music and film, working with industry-standard DAWs such as Pro Tools and Reaper. His focus lies in shaping emotionally rich soundscapes that enhance storytelling and fully immerse the audience
Live Sound Mixing
Equipped with strong knowledge of both digital and analog systems, he confidently operates a wide range of hardware and mixing consoles. His live work is known for delivering impactful, high-quality sound that elevates performances and captivates listeners
Technical Expertise in Audio Equipment & Instruments
With years of hands-on experience, Ngan Hong brings deep technical knowledge of audio gear and musical instruments. His quick troubleshooting skills, ability to calibrate, and even repair equipment on the fly make him an indispensable asset on any production

Highlights

Lead audio engineer for both AOrchestra and Crescendo Concert
Live sound mixing for Malaysia Jazz Piano Festival, Zee Avi, and Hoholand
Final mix for iQiyi’s film “Restu”
